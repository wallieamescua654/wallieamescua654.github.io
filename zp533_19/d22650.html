Plug and Play
Plug the mini receiver into the mic socket of any powered speaker, mixer, amplifier, digital recorder or some DLSR cameras for audio output, power on the mini receiver and microphone, it is ready to go. Use the 3.5mm to 1/4" adaptor if your audio device is built in 3.5mm mic socket instead of 1/4" socket.
**Note: we do not recommend to use this mic with laptop PCs or smartphones even with the adaptor.

No more cable tangle, more freedom
Battery powered microphone and receiver gives you the utmost freedom to walk in the room without the hassle and restriction of cables holding you back. The microphone is powered by 2XAA batteries and the Receiver is powered by rechargeable lithium battery.

Important notice

2.Please do not hold the bottom of the mic where the antenna is positioned to avoid signal transmission.

4. We do not recommend to use with portable bluetooth speaker lower than 15 watt, such as JBL charge, Doss touch.
