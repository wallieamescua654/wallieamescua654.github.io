説明: 2-Person Family Camping Dome Backpacking Tent

Explore the great outdoors and setup camp with ease in this new 2-Person Family Camping Dome Tent. Great for sleeping up to two, this tent is ideal for a quick and easy camping trip. Equipped with easy setup poles, stakes, and rain fly for unexpected weather, the Backpacking Tent is ready to help you take on your next vacation or adventure outdoors.

- Great for the warm or cool weather, and is coated to help repel water
- Polyethylene binding floor & Polyester rain fly provide water and wind resistance
- Two pole assembly with shock-corded fiberglass poles make setup
- Four l ground pegs for those breezy days
- Open mesh roof area provides excellent cross ventilation throughout
- Two-Way Zippers for easy use from inside or out
- Insect Screen with Three Zippers
- Fast and easy set up/breakdown with no tools required
- Lightweight at 2.60LB for hiking and backpacking
- Portable with a zipper carry bag, pole bag, stakes and assembly instructions
