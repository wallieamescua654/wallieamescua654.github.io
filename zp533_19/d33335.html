【本の内容】
Contemporary architecture presents itself with various aesthetical standards. Besides the rational function of architectural design architects now pay more attention to continuity from the inside to the outside of architecture. As a new way of expression it is a new concern in the field of contemporary architectural design. This book has collected a number of masterpieces that reflect the trend of modern architectural surface design. It has been divided into six chapters based on building materials. Here each project is presented with photographs and details of the nodes that objectively express the ideas and concepts of the architects.
