説明: Device Connection
1.Long press each of the three devices separately for three seconds to power on, after power on the receiver will automatically connect with the transmitter.
2.The blue indicator light (LINE) is always on, indicating that the connection is successful.
3.The blue indicator light (LINE) keeps flashing, indicating that the connection between the transmitter and the receiver has failed. Please click the transmitter power button and receiver power button respectively three times to enter the matching state.

Recording
Before recording, the transmitter needs to insert a memory card. After inserting the memory card, open the recording key (near REC) to record. The REC indicator remains on when the device is recording.

Audio Transmission
This product only supports external memory card to store data. To read the data, you need to put the external storage card into the card reader for data transfer.

Usage Scenarios ( Transmitter )

1.Using the built-in microphone
The transmitter can be fixed to clothing or a stable surface.Point the built-in microphone in the direction of the sound source.

2.Using an external microphone
Clip the transmitter onto the belt, and then clip the radio head of the collar clip microphone onto the collar. The microphone points in the direction of the sound source.
