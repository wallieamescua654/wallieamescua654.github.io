Globalizing Language Policy and Planning: An Irish Language Perspective

著者名: Mairead Moriarty 出版者: Palgrave Macmillan 出版年: 29-Jul-15 版表示: 1st ed. 2015 シリーズ: HardbackThe book examines the changing relationship between minority languages and language policy and planning in the context of globalization, through an examination of the Irish language context. It demonstrates how localized practices are involved in the refashioning of the value of the Irish language.
