商品コメント Multiーsized fins, ideal for sharing among family and friends or for rental departments. Designed for snorkeling, scuba diving, long swims.
Short lightweight and responsive blade. Easyーkicking blade responds well to any kicking style.
Blade extends from the top of the foot pocket for maximum surface area and for increase the propulsion force.
Fins stays in place against your foot despite the increase in hydro static pressure.
The fin pocket is made from a soft elastomer for added comfort. Made in Italy.
The Palau SAF is designed and manufactured in Italy by Cress, an Italian brand pioneer in scuba diving, snorkeling and swimming equipment since 1946.
