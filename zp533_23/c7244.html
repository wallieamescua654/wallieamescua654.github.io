Nonprofits are not like other businesses. They’re special. It doesn"t matter if you"re launching a career as part of a multi-million dollar organization or a volunteer running your local little league you"ll need special know-how to navigate the accounting practices and funding needs of a not- for-profit.
Nonprofit Management All-in-One For Dummies
is your guide for know-how on making a nonprofit organization operate properly. This beginner-friendly reference helps replace your shelf of nonprofit how-to books with a single reference to answer your questions on how to manage a mission-focused organization build budgets and raise funds while staying within the confines of the laws governing nonprofits. You"ll also find advice on valuable skills like marketing that benefit your organization.

This 5-books- in-1 Dummies guide is excellent for volunteers who step up to run a nonprofit that"s near and dear to their hearts or would-be professional non-profit managers who need advice on how to manage and grow an organization.
New feature
Build a diverse set of management skills
Managing a nonprofit organization requires a person who’s part people manager part fund-raiser part accountant part spokesperson and part marketer. This book covers all those requirements teaching you the ins and outs of the various aspects of nonprofit leadership. Inside you’ll find clear information on how to further the goals of the nonprofit build budgets and raise funds while staying compliant with regulations. Whether you’re president of your kids’ youth sports organization or planning a career running multimillion-dollar nonprofits there’s no better reference for learning the essentials than this one.
