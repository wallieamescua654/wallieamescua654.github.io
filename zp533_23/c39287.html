Unreal Houses: Character, Gender, and Genealogy in the Tale of Genji

著者名: Edith Sarra 出版者: Harvard University Press 出版年: 14 Apr 2020 版表示: シリーズ: HardbackEdith Sarra radically rethinks the Tale of Genji by focusing on the figure of the house-both the narrative"s images of aristocratic mansions and its representation of their inhabitants. Unreal Houses opens new perspectives on the architectonics of the Genji and the feminine milieu that midwifed what has been called the world"s first novel.
